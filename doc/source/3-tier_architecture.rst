====================
Nordlys architecture
====================

Nordlys is based on a `multitier architecture <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multitier_architecture>`_ with three layers:

  - **core** (*data* tier)
  - **logic**
  - **services** (*presentation* tier)
